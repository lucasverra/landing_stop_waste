ABOUT US

This project is the fruit of the collaboration seeded in a Carrefour data Hackaton, late september 2016. Without knowing each other previously, the team was founded out of the necesity of fighting the major problem of global food chain : waste.
STOP-WASTE is pushing the boundaries of tech in order to provide an elegant solution to a gigantic envoronmental problem.

The innovative aproach :

  • Work hand on hand with Carrefour experts
  • Treat inherent poor data quality with powerfull algorithms
  • Provide simple, user-friendly SaaS solution to better predict fresh producs demand
  • ...and STOP-WASTE
